BOOK COLLECTION

Discover your interests through a wide variety of genres available in our library collection. Each genre offers unique stories, knowledge, and perspectives to help you explore new ideas, expand your understanding, and find books that truly match your reading preferences.

ROMANCE

Romance focuses on love and relationships, showing how characters develop emotional connections, face challenges, and grow together through their romantic journey.

THE BEACH HOUSE
by Rachel Hanna
4.3

When Julie's life falls apart after her husband's betrayal, she starts over by buying an old cottage in the South Carolina Lowcountry. With unexpected family problems and the help of Dawson, a quiet contractor with a troubled past, Julie begins to rebuild her life.

BITTER HOUSE
by Kiersten Modglin
4.2

After inheriting Bitter House from her cold grandmother, Bridget returns to the manor she was once forced to leave. Living with Cole Warner, a man from her troubled past, she soon uncovers letters that reveal dark secrets hidden within the house.

THE HOUSE IN THE CERULEAN SEA
by TJ Klune
4.6

Linus Baker, a strict case worker for magical children, is sent to investigate an orphanage housing six dangerous youths. There, he meets Arthur Parnassus, the orphanage's devoted master, and slowly uncovers secrets that challenge everything he believes.

A SEAGROVE CHRISTMAS
by Rachel Hanna
4.5

During Christmas in Seagrove, Julie Lancaster plans a perfect holiday with her family, but the arrival of a mysterious woman changes everything. As secrets from the past surface and relationships are tested.

FULL HOUSE
(Nur Sakhira)
4.3

Billie Pearce lives a calm and predictable life until she meets Nicholas Kaharchek, a charming polo instructor who brings excitement and trouble with him. As their attraction grows, hidden danger begins to close in.

THE FOUR WINDS
by Kristin Hannah
4.6

Set during the Great Depression, The Four Winds follows Elsa Wolcott as she fights to protect her family and their land during one of America's hardest times. As drought, poverty, and hardship threaten everything she loves.
